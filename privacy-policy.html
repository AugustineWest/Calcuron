Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Calcuron (“the Site”). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. Calcuron is committed to transparency and simplicity. This site was intentionally designed to operate without accounts, logins, or stored personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Calcuron does not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Names
  • Addresses
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Date of birth
  • Financial information
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

All calculators and tools operate entirely within your browser. Your input data is never transmitted, stored, or saved on any server.

2. Local-Only Processing

All calculations (age, BMI, unit conversions, loan calculations, etc.) occur locally on your device using JavaScript. Your inputs remain private and are never sent to Calcuron or any third party.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Calcuron does not use:

  • Cookies
  • Local storage tracking
  • Browser fingerprinting
  • Analytics scripts (unless added in the future)

If Google AdSense is added to the site at a later time, Google may use cookies or other mechanisms as described below.

4. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

If Calcuron displays ads through Google AdSense, Google may use cookies (including DART cookies) to:

  • Serve personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Limit repetitive ads
  • Measure ad effectiveness

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visits to this site and/or other sites across the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

https://www.google.com/settings/ads

5. Third-Party Links

Calcuron may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. Please review the privacy policies of any website you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

Calcuron does not knowingly collect or store personal data from children under 13. Because we do not store any data at all, the site is generally safe for all ages.

7. Data Security

Because Calcuron stores no user data, there is no stored information to be breached. The site uses secure HTTPS connections to ensure encrypted communication between your browser and the site.

8. International Users (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Calcuron is inherently compliant with GDPR and CCPA principles because:

  • We store no personal data
  • We do not track users
  • We do not sell data
  • We do not use cookies

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. All updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

contact@calcuron.com

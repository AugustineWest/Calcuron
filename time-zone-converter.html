Time Zone Converter

Convert a specific date and time from your device’s current time zone into another time zone. Handy for calls, flights, and remote meetings. Everything runs locally in your browser.

This is interpreted in your device's current time zone (local time).
Formatting preference only; doesn’t change the actual time.
Converted time
Select a time and click “Convert Time”.
Once you enter a local date and time, you’ll see the exact equivalent in the target time zone, along with both zones’ UTC offsets at that moment (including daylight savings if applicable).
How this converter works
  • Uses your browser’s current time zone as the starting point.
  • Uses the selected IANA time zone (e.g., America/New_York) as the target.
  • Relies on your device’s time zone & daylight saving settings for accuracy.

For critical schedules (e.g., flights, international meetings), always double-check with official sources. System time, daylight savings rules, or browser quirks can occasionally differ.

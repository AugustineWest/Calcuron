Chess Rating Calculator

Estimate your new chess rating using the Elo formula after a win, loss, or draw against a specific opponent. All calculations run locally in your browser—your inputs never leave your device.

Calculate your new Elo rating

This calculator uses the standard Elo update formula: Rnew = Rold + K × (S − E), where S is the actual score (1 = win, 0.5 = draw, 0 = loss) and E is the expected score based on the rating difference.

Common K-values: 10 (masters), 20 (experienced), 32 (rapid improvement), 40 (provisional).
Enter both ratings, choose the game result, and tap “Calculate New Rating”.

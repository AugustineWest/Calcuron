Fraction Simplifier

Enter a fraction and instantly see it reduced to lowest terms, along with a mixed-number version (if needed) and a decimal approximation. All calculations run directly in your browser.

Use whole numbers (integers). Negative signs are allowed on the numerator or denominator.

This is a rough decimal approximation. Fractions like 2/3 repeat forever (0.6666…), so we round for display.
How fraction simplification works
  • We compute the greatest common divisor (GCD) of the numerator and denominator.
  • Then we divide both numerator and denominator by that GCD to get lowest terms.
  • If the fraction is improper (numerator larger than denominator), we show a mixed number too.
  • If the denominator is negative, the sign is moved to the numerator.

This tool doesn’t handle symbolic algebra—just numeric fractions. It’s useful for homework, recipes, and quick mental math checks.

How this converter works

All values are converted internally through milliliters (ml) using standard US recipe approximations:

  • 1 teaspoon = 4.93 ml
  • 1 tablespoon = 3 teaspoons = 14.79 ml
  • 1 fl oz ≈ 29.57 ml
  • 1 cup (US) = 240 ml (recipe rounded)
  • 1 liter = 1000 ml

For most home cooking and baking, these approximations are accurate enough. For professional baking or very delicate recipes, use a kitchen scale and ingredient-specific gram conversions.

This is a rough estimate using simple percentages. Actual paychecks depend on your W-4, filing status, tax brackets, pre-tax vs. post-tax deductions, and your employer’s payroll system.
What this calculator does (and doesn’t) do
  • Converts salary or hourly pay into an annual gross income.
  • Splits that annual income into paychecks based on pay frequency.
  • Applies a single combined percentage for taxes and deductions to approximate take-home pay.
  • Does not model tax brackets, allowances, credits, or complex pre-tax/post-tax rules.

Use this as a back-of-the-envelope estimate. For official numbers, check your pay stub or use your employer’s detailed payroll tools.

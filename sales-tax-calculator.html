Sales Tax Calculator

Quickly calculate sales tax and final total from a subtotal and tax rate, or reverse-calc the subtotal when you only know the final price including tax.

Enter your local sales tax rate, like 8.75 for 8.75%.
Results
Choose a mode, enter either the subtotal or the final total, then tap Calculate to see the breakdown.
This tool assumes a single percentage rate applied to the taxable subtotal. Actual store receipts may include additional local fees.
What this calculator shows
  • Subtotal – price before tax.
  • Sales tax amount – tax collected on the transaction.
  • Final total – what you pay after tax.

Forward mode:

  • Tax = Subtotal × (Tax rate ÷ 100).
  • Total = Subtotal + Tax.

Reverse mode (when you only know the total with tax):

  • Subtotal = Total ÷ (1 + Tax rate ÷ 100).
  • Tax = Total − Subtotal.

Actual tax rules can be more complex for shipping, discounts, or multiple jurisdictions. This calculator is a simple estimate for everyday use.

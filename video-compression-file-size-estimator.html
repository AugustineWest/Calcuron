How these estimates work

This tool uses a simplified bits-per-pixel (bpp) model scaled by resolution, frame rate, codec efficiency, and quality preset:

  • Higher resolutions and frame rates require more bits to maintain comparable quality.
  • More efficient codecs (like HEVC and AV1) can reach similar quality at 30–45% lower bitrates than baseline H.264.
  • Audio bitrate is added on top, and becomes more significant for long-form or multi-hour projects.

For professional delivery (broadcast, DCP, strict streaming specs), always follow the official guidelines from your platform, studio, or client.

Important notes
  • Actual degree requirements vary by country, institution, and accreditation body.
  • Use this as a brainstorming tool for planning and comparing paths, not as a formal academic guide.
  • Always confirm details with an official college or university catalog and an academic advisor.

Typical timelines: Associate ≈ 2 years (full time), Bachelor ≈ 4 years, Master ≈ 1–2 years after a bachelor’s degree, depending on program structure and course load.

Important notes

BMI is a rough screening tool, not a diagnosis. It does not directly measure body fat and may be less accurate for athletes, very muscular individuals, older adults, or people with certain health conditions.

For personal health decisions, always consider talking with a healthcare professional who can interpret BMI along with other factors such as waist circumference, blood pressure, labs, and medical history.
